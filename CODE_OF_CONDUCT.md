# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

As the maintainer and contributors of UrbanPulse AI, we pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or personal identity. 

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community around this academic project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
* Focusing on what is best for the project and the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope & Enforcement

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

As a single-maintainer project, enforcement is straightforward. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at:

**a.hadikidwai@gmail.com**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. If a participant engages in harassing behavior, I may take any action I deem appropriate, up to and including a temporary or permanent ban from the repository.
